The Clove Experience

The Clove Experience is a unique experience. It’s the convenience offered by clinic hours that suit your busy life. Saturday evening? Sunday morning? No problem – we’re open. It’s a friendly greeting from your dentist (not a receptionist!) as you enter the clinic. That’s what “Direct to Dentist” means: you’ll only interact with your dentist, because that’s who you’ve come to see. It’s technology that makes your experience pleasant. That’s right – pleasant. Because we use computer-guided injection technology that significantly reduces treatment-related pain. It’s the security in knowing that hygiene, sterilization and transparency are taken seriously. So seriously, that we’ve earned a membership in the UK’s Royal Society of Medicine. And we’re the only Indian healthcare company to have achieved this. Finally, it’s the care that follows once you’ve left the clinic. Your dentist will follow up to see how you’re doing. Because our care doesn’t end until we’re sure you’re feeling better.
